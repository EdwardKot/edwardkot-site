About

Edward Kot
Photographer

Based in Milan, working across Europe. Edward Kot's photography is drawn to the space between — the quiet interval before something begins, or after it ends.

His work spans editorial, commercial, and personal documentary — rooted in a sensitivity to light, atmosphere, and the subtle gestures that pass unnoticed.

Clients and collaborators include brands, agencies, and publications operating across fashion, culture, and lifestyle.

Available for commissions across Europe
