About Edward Kot

Photographer &
Mobile Imaging Specialist

Edward Kot is a Milan-based photographer working across Europe. He specializes in official smartphone camera sample photography, street lifestyle imagery and commercial visual assignments.

He has produced camera sample photography for Xiaomi, OPPO and realme across multiple device launches. His images have been presented at official product launch events, translating mobile imaging technology into credible photographs made in real European locations.

Alongside mobile imaging work, his practice spans editorial and personal documentary photography, with an observational approach to light, atmosphere and everyday gestures.
