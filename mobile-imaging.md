# Smartphone Sample Photography for Mobile Brands

Edward Kot creates refined, cinematic, real-world image samples for mobile
brands, imaging teams, and creative agencies. The work sits between
photographic storytelling and product communication: images need to feel
natural while clearly serving a launch, campaign, or camera feature story.

Since 2022, Edward has created sample photography for dozens of pre-launch
smartphones, including early work for flagship imaging programs such as
Xiaomi x Leica, OPPO x Hasselblad, and realme x Ricoh.

His images have been used in product launch events, offline campaigns, social
media, and camera feature showcases.

Edward translates product briefs, camera features, and marketing objectives
into executable production decisions: scouting locations, designing shooting
routes, sourcing models, reading light and timing, and making images that
communicate both technical capability and emotional atmosphere.

The goal is not to create a technical test chart. The goal is to make
photographs that feel credible in real life while carrying the visual message a
product launch needs.
