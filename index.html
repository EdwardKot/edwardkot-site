Approach

Mobile imaging, street photography, and real-world visual storytelling

Edward Kot works at the intersection of mobile imaging, street photography, and real-world visual storytelling.

He translates product briefs, camera features, and marketing objectives into photographs that actually work — scouting locations, designing shooting routes, sourcing models, reading light and timing, and making images that communicate both technical capability and emotional atmosphere.

His photography is grounded in real life, human moments, cinematic atmosphere, and a refined sense of place.

Experience

Recognition and selected experience

Since 2022, Edward has created sample photography for dozens of pre-launch smartphones, including early work for flagship imaging programs such as Xiaomi × Leica, OPPO × Hasselblad, and realme × Ricoh. His images have been used in product launch events, offline campaigns, and camera feature showcases.

His work also spans editorial, fashion, and documentary assignments, including magazine projects and Milan Fashion Week coverage.
