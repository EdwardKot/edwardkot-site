Mobile Imaging

Smartphone sample photography for mobile brands

Edward Kot creates refined, cinematic, real-world image samples for mobile brands, imaging teams, and creative agencies. The work sits between photographic storytelling and product communication: images need to feel natural while clearly serving a launch, campaign, or camera feature story.

Experience

Images that make mobile camera capability visible and credible

Since 2022, Edward has created sample photography for dozens of pre-launch smartphones, including early work for flagship imaging programs such as Xiaomi × Leica, OPPO × Hasselblad, and realme × Ricoh.

His images have been used in product launch events, offline campaigns, social media, and camera feature showcases.

Approach

From brief to photographs that actually work

Edward translates product briefs, camera features, and marketing objectives into executable production decisions: scouting locations, designing shooting routes, sourcing models, reading light and timing, and making images that communicate both technical capability and emotional atmosphere.

The goal is not to create a technical test chart. The goal is to make photographs that feel credible in real life while carrying the visual message a product launch needs.
